Haiku describing Chewbacca's perfections.    
        漢字で。。。英語なし

Here’s the English meaning of the haiku:

Original (Japanese):
漆黒毛並
森を震わす咆哮
忠義の友よ

Translation (English):
Jet-black fur gleams,
A roar that shakes the forest—
Loyal friend always.

It keeps the spirit of Chewbacca: powerful, wild, and deeply loyal.
